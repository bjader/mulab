Get to know your experiment

Use our selection of videos, interactive demonstrations and written theory to understand your apparatus, as well as what you will be testing, before you even step into the lab.

Simulate results

Our interactive simulations allow you to vary parameters, providing instantaneous feedback with simulated results based on your inputs. This allows you to find ideal values for the parameters, fine tuning your equipment and saving you time in the lab.

Analyse your data

Using our dedicated analysis tools that have been specifically designed for your experiment to save time manipulating, calculating and graphing your results.
