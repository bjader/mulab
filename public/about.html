What is muLab?

muLab is the first phase of a project that aims to improve the learning experience in undergraduate science programmes. Our interactive laboratory software offers simulations based on the advanced 3rd year labs in the Experimental Physics module at University College London. With realistic generation of raw data, students can vary parameters and conditions to observe the effect it has on the data and the final results. If you want to know more, what does muLab actually do?

muLab also consists of an open bank of high quality student resources. Each simulated experiment is accompanied by a clear theory page and a thorough assessment quiz, meant to ensure the student gets the complete benefit of using the online laboratory. These resources are not designed to replace the learning process, but rather to compliment existing laboratory scripts with information tailored by the feedback of previous students.

Motivation

As third year physics students, we understand lab work is an essential part of a Physics degree. Thats why we've worked hard to produce software that streamlines the lab process without detracting from the difficulty of the work.

By familiarising students with the experiments and equipment ahead of time, less time is wasted adjusting parameters before the experiment. This enables more time to be given to data collection and analysis.

About us
